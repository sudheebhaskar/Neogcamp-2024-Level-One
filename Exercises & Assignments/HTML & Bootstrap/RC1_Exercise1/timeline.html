Key events in the life of Bill Gates

  • 1955: Bill Gates is born on October 28 in Seattle, Washington.
  • 1975: Co-founds Microsoft with Paul Allen
  • 1980: Microsoft signs a deal with IBM to provide an operating system (MS-DOS) for their first personal computer.
  • 1985: Windows 1.0 is launched, marking the beginning of Microsoft's dominance in the operating system market.
  • 1995: Windows 95 is released, introducing the Start menu and a new era of for personal computing.
  • 2000: Bill snd Melinda Gates establish the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation.
  • 2008:Bill Gates transitions from his day-to-day role at Microsoft to focus more on philanthropy.
  • 2010:Gates, along with Warren Buffet, launches the Giving Pledge, encouraging billionaires to commit the majority of their wealth to philanthropy.
  • 2014: Gates step down as Chairman of Microsoft, continuing to serve as a technology advisor
  • 2021: Bill and Melinda Gates announce their divorce.
  • Present: Bill Gates continues to be actively involved in philanthropy and addresses global challenges through various initiatives.
