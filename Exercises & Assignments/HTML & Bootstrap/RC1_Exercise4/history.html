History of Mumbai City

Mumbai, formerly known as Bombay, has a rich history that dates back centuries. Here are key milestones that shaped the city:

Ancient Period

Mumbai has evidence of human habitation dating back to the Stone Age. The city was a collection of islands inhabitated by the Koli fishing community.

Independence and Post-Independence

Mumbai played a crucial role in India's struggle for independence. After gaining freedom in 1947, Mumbai became the capital of the newly formed state of Maharashtra in 1960.

Economic Hub

Over the years, Mumbai evolved into the financial, commercial, and entertainment capital of India. The city's skyline is dotted with skyscrapers, and it continues to be a melting pot of diverse cultures and communities.
