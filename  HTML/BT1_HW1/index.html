Overview

Virat Kohli, born on Novemebr 1988, in Delhi, India is one of the worlds's leading cricketers. He is known for his aggressive batting and strong leadership as the captain of Indian cricket team.

Kohli has consitently ranked among the world's top batsmen and has received numerous awards and accolades for his remarkable cricketing skills.
