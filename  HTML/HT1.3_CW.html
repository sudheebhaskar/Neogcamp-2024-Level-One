Festivals

Diwali, Festival of Lights, is India's most celebrated festivals

Places to visit

Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is an iconic symbolf of love and a UNESCO world heritage site.

Jaipur

Jaipur, often referred to as the Pink city is known for its historic sites, including:

  • Amer Fort
  • Hawa Mahal
  • City Palace

Kerala

Kerala is famous for its serene backwaters and lush green landscapes

Indian Cuisine

Butter Chicken

Butter Chicken is a creamy and flavorful Indian dish.

Biryani

Biryani is a fragrant and aromatic roce dish with various regional variations. Notable ingredients include:

  1. Basmati Rice
  2. Chicken or Lamb (Vegetables for a vegetarian version)
  3. Spices (Cumin, coriander, and garam masala)
  4. Yogurt and saffron for flavor and color

Dosa

Dosa is a South Indian crispy pancake made with fermented rice and lentil batter.
