Biography

William Henry Gates III, commonly known as Bill Gates, is an American business magnate, software developer, and philanthropist. Born on October 28, 1995, in Seattle, Washington. Gates co-founded Microsoft and played a crucial role in the personal computer revolution.

Microsoft and Software

Bill Gates co-founded Microsoft in 1975 and led the company to develop software products that became industry-standard. Microsoft's Windows operatin system and office suite are among the most widely used worldwide.

Entrepreneurship

Philanthropy
