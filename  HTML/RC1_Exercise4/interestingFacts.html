Interesting Facts about Mumbai City

Dabbawalas

Mumbai's dabbawalas are known for their efficient lunchbox delivery system, with a Six Sigma certification for accuracy.

Bollywood

Mumbai is the heart of the Indian Film industry, commonly known as Bollywood, commonly known as Bollywood, producing numerous films each year

Elephanta Caves

The Elephanta Caves, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, feature rock-cut caves dedicated to Hindu and Buddhist deities.
